Guests

- All guests of pool members will be charged $3/day to swim and must be accompanied by a current pool member

- Guest fees can be sent via Venmo (@Whitmarpool). The venmo payment info is displayed at the pool near the sign-in sheet.

- All guests must sign-in and sign-out upon entry/exit of the pool

Pool Rules

- Swim at your own Risk. No exceptions

- Keep the gate locked at all times, for your safety.

- Pool is for members only. Members can bring up to 4 guests at a time ($3/guest). Guest fee can be paid via Venmo. (@Whitmarpool)

- All members and guests must sign-in and sign-out

- No diving off shallow end.

- No running or rough play

- No glass

- No pets

- No smoking, alcohol, or drugs

- no pottying in the Pool

- No yelling, profanity, or loud music

- No solo swimming

- No public show of affection.

- Watch your children

- Don't litter

- No gum chewing or food in the Pool

- Person's under the age of 14 must be accompanied by an adult

- Management reserves the right to deny use of the pool to anyone at any time
